- [Supported YouTube file formats](https://support.google.com/youtube/troubleshooter/2888402)
- [Recommended upload encoding settings](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/1722171)
    - Container: MP4
    - Audio codec: AAC-LC
        - Channels: Stereo or Stereo + 5.1
        - Sample rate 96khz or 48khz
    - Video codec: H.264
        - Progressive scan (no interlacing)
        - Chroma subsampling: 4:2:0
    - Frame rate
        - same frame rate it was recorded
    - Bitrate
        - SDR video
            - 1080p
                - for Standard Frame Rate: 8 Mbps
                - for High Frame Rate: 12 Mbps
            - 720p
                - for Standard Frame Rate: 5 Mbps
                - for High Frame Rate: 7.5 Mbps
        - audio
            - Stereo: 384 kbps
            - 5.1: 512 kbps
    - Resolution and aspect ratio
        - The standard aspect ratio for YouTube on a computer is 16:9.
    - Color Space
